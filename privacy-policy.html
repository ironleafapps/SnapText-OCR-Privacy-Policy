Privacy Policy for SnapText OCR

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Developed by Ironleaf Apps

SnapText OCR ("the App," "we," "our") is developed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and confirms what data we do not collect.

1. Summary

SnapText OCR performs all text recognition directly on your device using Google ML Kit. No images, PDFs, or extracted text are uploaded to any server, and the App does not require an internet connection to function.

2. Information We Access

The App requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera: Used to capture photos of documents for text recognition. Images are processed on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.
  • Photos / Gallery / Storage: Used to let you select existing images or PDF files for text extraction.

None of this content ever leaves your device. We do not have servers that receive, store, or process your images, PDFs, or extracted text.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
  • We do not upload your images, PDFs, or scanned text to any cloud service
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or behavioral tracking
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

SnapText OCR uses Google ML Kit for on-device text recognition. ML Kit's text recognition model runs locally on your device and does not send your images, photos, or extracted text to Google's servers. The underlying SDK may collect limited anonymous diagnostic data (such as API performance metrics and crash reports) to help Google maintain the service — this never includes the content of your scans. For more information, see Google ML Kit's Terms of Service.

5. Data Storage

Any images, cropped photos, or extracted text created while using the App are stored locally on your device only, within the App's own storage or wherever you choose to save/export/share them (e.g., clipboard, WhatsApp, Email). We have no access to this data.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapText OCR does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since the App collects no personal data at all, it is safe for use by all ages in this respect.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
